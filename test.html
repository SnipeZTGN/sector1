About the Authors

The two authors and editors of all the current blogs are known to us as Alfio Laguza and Mason Potts (although Alfio likes to be called Alfie). They are both creative young people who study at Patrician Brother's College Fairfield, who enjoy coding, gaming and sports. Although Mason is a lot better at coding than Alfie, thanks to his uncle, this is both our first OFFICIAL website. With the many difficulties that we have both encountered, we have taken a leap over them, and have succeeded. This website is a great step for us, and believe that we have both completed a fascinating, interesting and fun website that all people, young and old, will enjoy!
